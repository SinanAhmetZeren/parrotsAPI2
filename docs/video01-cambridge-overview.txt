VIDEO 01 — INTRO / OVERVIEW (Cambridge)
========================================
Setting: Cambridge, UK — River Cam, punting, college architecture


CONCEPT
-------
Use Cambridge as the demo city. Visually strong, internationally recognizable,
English-speaking audience. The Cam river + punting is a natural fit for the
boat owner and tourist personas.


SCREENSHOTS / SCREEN RECORDINGS TO CAPTURE
-------------------------------------------
1. Home screen — map centered on Cambridge, markers visible on the Cam
2. Voyage card — a punting tour along the river (attractive cover photo)
3. Voyage detail — waypoints at King's College, The Backs, Grantchester Meadows
4. Captain's public profile — punting photo, short bio
5. Bid flow — tourist placing a bid on the punting tour
6. Chat screen — realistic message exchange between captain and traveler


SEEDING THE APP FOR SCREENSHOTS
--------------------------------
- Create a voyage along the Cam with real-looking waypoints
- Add enough voyages around Cambridge so the map doesn't look empty
- Use a real punting/river photo as the voyage cover image
- Write realistic profile bio and voyage description in English


VIDEO STRUCTURE (2-3 min)
--------------------------
1. Parrots logo bumper (logo + music)
2. Map opens on Cambridge — markers appear on the river
3. Tap a voyage — punting tour card expands
4. Scroll waypoints — King's College, The Backs, Grantchester Meadows
5. Check captain's profile
6. Tourist places a bid
7. Captain accepts — chat opens
8. Call to action — download link / app store badge


NOTES
-----
- Keep each clip 3-5 seconds, cut fast with music underneath
- AI voiceover explaining what Parrots is and why it exists
- Same intro bumper used on all persona videos for brand consistency
- English-language narration targets global audience
