USER PERSONAS
=============

1. Boat or Vehicle Owner
   Use Case: Wants to rent out his vehicle to a group of people during a certain time period.
   - CreateVehicleScreen: Create vehicle
   - CreateVoyageScreen: Create voyage
   - VoyageDetailScreen: Accept bids
   - ConversationDetailScreen: Go to messaging & share additional details

2. Boat Broker
   Use Case: Wants to rent out other captains' boats and receive commissions.
   - EditProfileScreen: Edit profile screen & add social media
   - CreateVehicleScreen: Add other captains' vehicles
   - ProfileScreen: Share profile on social media profiles

3. Car Enthusiast
   Use Case: Wants to showcase her car to friends.
   - EditProfileScreen: Edit profile screen & add social media
   - CreateVehicleScreen: Add vehicles
   - ProfileScreen: Share profile with friends

4. Visiting Tourist
   Use Case: Visiting a city and wants to check out tours originating from nearby.
   - HomeScreen: Search voyages
   - VoyageDetailScreen: See voyage details & waypoints
   - ProfileScreenPublic: Check out user's profile
   - ProfileScreenPublic: Check out other attending users' profiles
   - VoyageDetailScreen: Create bid

5. Group of Friends on the Move
   Use Case: Want to attend a certain tour guide's event.
   - ProfileScreenPublic: Go to tour guide's public profile
   - ProfileScreenPublic: Check out Instagram
   - ConversationDetailScreen: Send message to tour guide

6. Social Good
   Use Case: Wants to raise money for social good and draw attention to a social issue.
   - CreateVoyageScreen: Create public running event
   - VoyageDetailScreen: Accept highest bids for fundraiser

7. Motorcycle Club
   Use Case: They want to go on a wild west motorcycle tour.
   - CreateVoyageScreen: Create open riding event
   - ConversationDetailScreen: Send DM via the app to other members

8. Restaurant Owner
   Use Case: Wants to advertise his newly opened restaurant to tourists.
   - VoyageDetailScreen: Creates a landing page popup
