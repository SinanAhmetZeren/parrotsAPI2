GOOGLE PLAY STORE & YOUTUBE PRESENCE
=====================================


PART 1 — GOOGLE PLAY STORE REGISTRATION
========================================

1. Create a Google Play Developer account ($25 one-time fee)
   https://play.google.com/console

2. Upload the APK

3. Fill store listing
   - App description
   - Screenshots
   - Category

4. Set content rating, pricing (free), target countries

5. Submit for review (~3-7 days)


PART 2 — YOUTUBE PRESENCE
==========================

VIDEO 1 — Intro / Overview (2-3 min)
--------------------------------------
- Show the app opening, the map, voyages on the map
- Quick walkthrough: what is Parrots, why it exists
- End with a call to action (download link)

VIDEO 2 — "I have a boat" (Boat Owner persona, 1-2 min)
---------------------------------------------------------
- Create a vehicle
- Post a voyage
- Accept a bid
- Chat with the traveler

VIDEO 3 — "I'm visiting Istanbul" (Tourist persona, 1-2 min)
-------------------------------------------------------------
- Search map
- Find a tour
- Check the captain's public profile
- Place a bid

VIDEO 4 — "We want to ride" (Motorcycle Club persona, 1-2 min)
---------------------------------------------------------------
- Create an open riding event
- Members message each other through the app

VIDEO 5 — "Good cause" (Social Good persona, 1-2 min)
------------------------------------------------------
- Create a fundraiser voyage
- Accept top bids

VIDEO 6 — "I own a restaurant" (Restaurant Owner persona, 1-2 min)
-------------------------------------------------------------------
- Create a place card that tourists discover on the map


FORMAT GUIDELINES
-----------------
- Screen recording of the app with voiceover
- Keep each video under 2 minutes
- Same intro bumper on every video (Parrots logo + music) for brand consistency
